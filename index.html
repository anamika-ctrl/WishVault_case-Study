Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built specifically for Indian shoppers who want to buy smarter — not spend more time shopping.

✦

One-click save

A floating "Save" button appears on every product page. Click once — title, price, image, and URL are captured instantly. No copy-pasting, no app switching.

📉

Price drop alerts

WishVault watches your saved items 24/7. The moment a price drops more than 5%, you get a push notification and email — before the deal ends.

⚖️

Compare across sites

See the same product's price on Amazon, Flipkart, Croma and more — side by side, in real time. Always know where to buy at the best price.

📊

Price history

Before you buy, see 90 days of price history. Know if today's "sale" is actually a deal, or if the product was cheaper last month.

🎁

Shareable gift lists

Share your wishlist via a simple link — no sign-up required to view. Friends can mark items as "claimed" so you never get duplicate birthday gifts.

📱

Synced everywhere

Save on Chrome at work, check on your phone at home. Your entire wishlist stays in sync across every device, instantly.
